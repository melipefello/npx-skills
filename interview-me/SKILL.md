name: interview-me
description: Use when the user wants to stress-test their thinking, get grilled on a plan or design, explore decision branches, or mentions "grill me" or "interview me". Works for any domain — software, business, strategy, or personal decisions.

Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the decision tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one. For each question, provide your recommended answer.

If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead.

> **Pipeline note:** This skill can be used standalone for critical thinking on any topic. When used as part of the spec pipeline, the next step is `/create-a-spec`.
